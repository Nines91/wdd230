Cordoba chamber of commerce site plan

Site Name

Cordoba Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.


Site Purpose

The Cordoba Chamber of Commerce acts as the representative and reference institution of the commercial sector that promotes the growth and prosperity of commercial activity in Cordoba. We look after the interests of the commercial sector, promoting development, competitiveness and entrepreneurial spirit through services, training and events.
